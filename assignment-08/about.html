Why I Chose This Instructable






First of all, this instructable is probably jumping more into CSS than was needed but I wanted to get some practice before the next module. I love fishing and consider myself an expert in some aspects of fishing. Every style of fishing (including the environment) is different, and it's extremely tough to master it all. This is my favorite way to tie on a lure or a fly, and it's simple enough of a process to get a beginner at fishing started on the right track.
